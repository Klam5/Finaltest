General Idea

League of Legends (also referred to as "LoL") is a 5v5 team based strategy game. There are about 141 playable characters, each with 4 unique skills. You play with your team and try to invade the opponents' base and destroy what is called their "nexus." Once a nexus is broken or a team surrenders, then the game will end.
